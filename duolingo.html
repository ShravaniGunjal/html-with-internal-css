frequently asked questions
Does Duolingo for Schools cost money?
No! Duolingo for Schools is 100% free.
Is Duolingo for Schools different from Duolingo?
Duolingo for Schools is a free layer of management that sits on top of the Duolingo language learning app. You’ll gain visibility into and a level of control over your students’ experience on Duolingo.
What if my students are already using Duolingo?
We’ve got you covered! Add existing accounts to your classroom to preserve existing student progress, or get students started fresh with our quick account setup.
